ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY

1. ENTITLEMENT
All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to standard public holidays. Part-time staff receive a pro-rata entitlement based on their contracted hours.

2. BOOKING PROCEDURE
Leave requests must be submitted via the HR Portal at least two weeks in advance. For leave exceeding 5 consecutive days, one month's notice is required. All requests are subject to manager approval based on departmental coverage.

3. CARRY-OVER RULES
Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused leave days into the next calendar year. These days must be used by March 31st of the following year, or they will be forfeited.

4. SICKNESS DURING LEAVE
If an employee falls ill during their annual leave, they can request to have those days reclassified as sick leave, provided a valid medical certificate is produced for the duration of the illness.
