IT SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION GUIDELINES

1. PASSWORD MANAGEMENT
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords should be changed every 90 days. Sharing passwords between employees is strictly prohibited.

2. REMOTE WORK SECURITY
When working from home or public spaces, employees must use the company-approved VPN. Do not use public Wi-Fi for accessing sensitive corporate databases without VPN protection.

3. PHYSICAL SECURITY
Laptops must be locked (Windows + L) whenever an employee leaves their desk. In the office, a clean desk policy is enforced; sensitive documents should not be left unattended and must be shredded at the end of the day.

4. EMAIL SAFETY
Be vigilant against phishing. Do not click on links or download attachments from unknown senders. Report any suspicious emails to the IT Security team immediately via the 'Report' button in Outlook.
