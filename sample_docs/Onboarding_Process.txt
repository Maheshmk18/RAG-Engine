EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING PROCESS

WELCOME TO THE TEAM!
Our onboarding process is designed to help you settle in quickly and feel productive.

DAY 1: ORIENTATION
- 09:00: Meet with HR for documentation and ID badge collection.
- 10:30: IT Equipment setup and login credentials walkthrough.
- 12:00: Welcome lunch with your immediate team.
- 14:00: Office tour and introduction to departmental leads.

WEEK 1: INTEGRATION
- Mandatory training: Complete the 'Code of Conduct' and 'GDPR Awareness' modules on the learning portal.
- Manager 1-on-1: Discuss your 30-60-90 day goals and key performance indicators.
- Social: Join the 'New Joiners' Slack channel to meet other colleagues.

MONTH 1: PROGRESS REVIEW
By the end of your first month, you should have a clear understanding of your role, completed all mandatory training, and submitted your first weekly progress report to your manager.

PROBATION PERIOD
The standard probation period is 6 months. A mid-point review will be conducted at the 3-month mark to discuss your performance and any support needed.
